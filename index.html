Description

Dreams For Tomorrow - a social non-profit and voluntary based organization that works towards the development of the less-fortunate rural school students of Bangladesh. We interact with high school students on regular basis and encourage them the to go beyond their standard school curriculum, acquaint them with computer-internet based knowledge, instill them skills to become leaders of tomorrow, inspire them to dream beyond the sky and light their inner spark with patriotism and values.
